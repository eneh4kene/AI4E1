Under UK GDPR, we process your data on the basis of legitimate interests (responding to your enquiry) and, where applicable, consent (if you opt into further communications). You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing hello@ai4e1.com.

Your rights

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@ai4e1.com. We will respond within 30 days.
